Firewatch

Central to the composition is the firewatch tower, standing as a lone sentinel against the sprawling landscape. This poster eschews intricate details, opting for clean lines and negative space, yet manages to convey the tower's significance in the narrative. A thin plume of smoke rises from the chimney, a poignant reminder of the character's isolation and the ever-present responsibility of watching for forest fires.
