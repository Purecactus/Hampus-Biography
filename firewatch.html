Firewatch poster

Against a backdrop of serene Wyoming wilderness, this poster employs a subdued color palette that mirrors the game's atmospheric and contemplative tone. The towering pine trees and distant mountain peaks are rendered with meticulous simplicity, creating a sense of both vastness and isolation. The hues of warm oranges and deep blues evoke the changing moods of the game's day-night cycle, subtly reflecting the passage of time.
